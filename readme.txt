== Description ==

= What is AdLemons =

[AdLemons](http://adLemons.com) is the first adversiting platform focused in blogs.    
If you are a blogger, with [AdLemons](http://adLemons.com) you can take control of ads on your blogs, and establish the price of your ad units monthly, so you will increase your incomes easily.
    
The AdLemons plugin will let you to add your zones directly on your Wordpress as widgets, so you can drag it on your sidebar easily.    

== Installation ==

You can directly search _adlemons_ in Wordpress > Extends > Plugins.


If you prefer install it manually, here we describe how to install the plugin and get it working.

1. Upload `adlemons.php` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Go to Appearance -> Widgets and drag your Adlemons ad units to your sidebar.
